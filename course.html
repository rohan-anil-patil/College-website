Computer Engineering

Computer engineering is a branch of electrical engineering and computer science that integrates several fields of computer science and electronic engineering required to develop computer hardware and software

Information Technology

Information technology (IT) is the use of computers to create, process, store, retrieve and exchange all kinds of data and information.

Artificial intelligence and data science

AI is the intelligence demonstrated by machines, while data science is an interdisciplinary field that uses statistics, scientific computing, methods, processes, algorithms and systems to extract knowledge and insights from data.

Computer science Engineering

Computer science engineering includes the study of design, computational processes and programming languages. Students get to learn technical know-how about how to develop a variety of hardwares and softwares and gain knowledge about communications systems and other similar devices.

Electronic and telecommunication Engineering

Electronics and telecommunication engineering is the design, fabrication, production, testing and operation of electronic devices, circuits, systems and networks.s

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering is an engineering branch that combines engineering physics and mathematics with materials science to design, analyze, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems.
