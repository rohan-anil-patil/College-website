About us

We are Konkan Gyanpeeth College

Konkan Gyanpeeth College of Engineering, Karjat was established 25 years ago out of the dream of Late Hon. Prabhakar Narayan (Appasaheb) Dharkar, Ex-Minister of Maharashtra Government. It offers undergraduate degree programs in six disciplines, with separate infrastructure for the laboratories of major subjects. The institute maintains a conducive and learning atmosphere, with student support facilities such as common room, bus transport, hostel, and large playground. Grievances are addressed in person through committees, and the institute promotes leadership qualities through sports and cultural events. Training and Placement Cell takes care of skill development and organises placement drives.
